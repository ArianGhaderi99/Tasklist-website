About TaskFlow

TaskFlow is a modern, lightweight task manager designed to help you organize your daily activities without any unnecessary complexity. Whether you're a student, professional, or anyone looking to boost productivity, TaskFlow provides a clean interface to manage your to-dos effectively.

Key Features

  • 📝 Task Creation – Add tasks with descriptions, due dates, and due times.
  • ✅ Completion Tracking – Mark tasks as done with a single click.
  • 🗑️ Easy Deletion – Remove tasks you no longer need.
  • 💾 Local Storage – Your tasks are saved in your browser, so no data loss on refresh.
  • 📱 Fully Responsive – Works seamlessly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • 🚀 No Account Required – Start using immediately without signup.

Why we built TaskFlow

Many task managers are overloaded with features and require accounts. We wanted a simple, privacy-friendly tool that does one thing well: help you remember and complete your tasks. TaskFlow stores everything locally on your device – no servers, no tracking.

Future Plans

We are planning to add categories, priority levels, and optional cloud sync. Stay tuned!
